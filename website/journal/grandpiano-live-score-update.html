GrandPiano live score and reading workflow update

Published 9 April 2026

GrandPiano is moving further toward a reading-first piano experience. The live score direction matters because it connects notation, keyboard interaction, rhythm awareness, and external MIDI practice inside one app workflow.

From an SEO perspective, pages like this create ongoing content around piano learning, sheet music, MIDI practice, and music reading. From a user perspective, they explain why the app is structured the way it is and where it is heading next.

The strongest long-term value comes from combining clear music education content with real updates that users can follow over time. That is why the journal exists alongside the main product and band pages.
