The Gyro platform roadmap for apps, books, and releases

Published 9 April 2026

The Gyro site is being structured as more than a landing page. It is intended to become the central public platform for the band, GrandPiano, future books, and future listening products. Search engines respond better when each topic has its own clear destination and internal links connect them coherently.

That is why the site now has dedicated pages for GrandPiano, books, audio player plans, and releases, plus a journal for ongoing updates. This gives the domain more indexable surface area and gives users better routes into the ecosystem.

The next natural layer is real release-specific content, more journal posts, and stronger cross-linking from official social profiles and external mentions.
